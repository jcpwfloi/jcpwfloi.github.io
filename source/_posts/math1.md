## Basic Algebraic Structures
### Group
Definition: A pair of a set G and a operation $\cdot$ on such set. All following equations hold for all $a, b, c \in G$

* $a\cdot(b\cdot c)=(a\cdot b)\cdot c$
* $a\cdot e=e\cdot a = a$
